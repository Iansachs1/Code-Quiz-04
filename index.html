Coding Quiz Challenge

This is a timed quiz to test your knowledge of javascript. Once you begin the quiz, you will have one minute and thirty seconds to answer as many questions as you can.

You gain 10 points for every correct answer, and one point for every second left on the clock after answering all questions.

For every incorrect answer, 20 seconds will be subtracted from the remaining time.
